Market Size

There is a substantial market size due to the strong demand for Asian cuisine in the U.S, Chinese in particular

  • In fact, more than 42% of American adults consume Chinese cuisine a few times a year
  • There are more than 40,000 Chinese restaurants in the U.S. This number is greater than all the McDonald's, KFCs, Pizza Huts, Taco Bells, and Wendy's locations in America combined.

Industry Outlook

There is massive room for growth in the industry: The U.S Chinese restaurant industry's revenue is increasing at an annualized rate of 2.9%, and is predicted to be worth up to 17.7 billion dollars in 2025. Additionally, the recent boom of delivery and ghost kitchens is bringing more technology into these traditional spaces and primes the industry for MiFoodTech (first graph). Additionally, with wages in the industry consistently rising (second graph), the need to adopt technology or change current recruiting strategies has only become more apparent.
